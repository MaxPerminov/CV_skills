-QC(control)Engineering improves product quality during product-developing process.
-QA(assurance)Engineering improves product quality, using methods of quality control
and improves testing processes by analyzing results of previous developing processes. 

What Does a QA Engineer Do

1)Testing process analysis
2)Testing process optimization
3)Documentation management
4)Analysis of testing
5)Planning of testing
6)Communication with developers
7)Test-case writing
8)Functional testing
9)Bug tracking, bug reporting
10)Confirmation testing
