1)what QA roles of game testing do you know

- Game QA: bug tracking and reporting, gameplay testing.
- Game producer, Release Manager: setting of estimations, quality requirements.
- Lead tester: coordinating QA with designers, developers, chiefs. Controlling of test
documentation.
- SDET (Software Development Engineer in Test), Technical Testers or Automation Testers:
performance and security.
- Play Testers: Gameplay.
- Coding testers.
- Hardware Testers.
- Localization Testers.
